Privacy Policy

What our goal is

Starlet ("Prevail Media Interactive, LLC") will always remain commited to safeguarding and protecting your privacy. All data that you provide on Starlet can be viewed and only developers at Prevail Media Interactive, LLC will be able to view it. In the future, if this ever changes, whether there's a company merger or attempted company or enterprise restructure that would require other entities than the currently approved to gain access in viewing your data, you will be made aware immediately. We want to give you a full understanding on what we do with your data, what type of data we collect, why we collect it and if there's anything else we do with it. Trust is important. And with the amount of data we use every single day through Starlet, it's one of our highest priorities. We honor that trust and we will continue to do more security measures in making sure that the data can only be viewed by approved parties that you've been made aware of.

Future modifications or potential revisions of this Privacy Policy

Prevail Media Interactive, LLC retains the prerogative to modify, amend, supplement, or retract statements of this Privacy Policy at our sole discretion. We would strongly recommend frequently revisiting and reviewing this Privacy Policy to stay informed.

Who can access Starlet data?

Starlet ("Prevail Media Interactive") follows the principle of least privilege, as such, higher-ups at Prevail Media Interactive, LLC do not need to access the data that you have willingly consented to share with Starlet. As of right now, the only parties with granted access to view and modify Starlet data are developers from Prevail Media Interactive, LLC. No other third-parties or services can access Starlet's database information or servers. If in the future this changes, you will be notified promptly.

Requesting my data or terminating my data from Starlet servers and services

To open a formal transparent request for the data and information that Starlet holds, you can initiate the process by contacting us at data@starlet.gg. Expected response times can vary from 12-30 days. Data formal transparent reports can also be provided through Discord in our support server.

Subscriptions

Starlet is a Discord bot that can operate in any Discord server but commands on Starlet cannot be used until a subscription is started. You can start your subscription on Starlet by using the /subscription command on Starlet. For subscriptions, we use a third-party payment provider called Stripe. You can read Stripe's privacy policy here. Once your subscription has started, we store your Stripe Subscription ID, the fact that the payment was processed through Stripe Subscription Payment Processor Type, the Discord User ID of the user who initiated the /subscription command and the Guild ID of the server. This data is stored in a secure database that only Prevail Media Interactive has access to. Once the subscription ends, this data will be deleted within 30 days. For more questions on subscriptions and your data, you can request a log of your subscription data in our databases by contacting us at subdata@starlet.gg. You should expect a response within 14 days. Note: If your data was already deleted before we were able to generate a response, we won't be able to provide you a log with your data. Data removal is an automatic process until requested otherwise.

What data do you collect

To create a safe and fair environment for the businesses, brands, content creators, streamers and musicians who and can come to use Starlet, we employ verification measures to enhance the user experience and security. This verification involves users placing unique randomized code identifiers on their social media pages. Upon successful verification, we collect and store the following data in our secure databases. The data in question is only saved in Starlet databases and servers. This data can all be requested through email at gdpr@starlet.gg. Expected responses can be 7 days.

  • TikTok Username: Users on Starlet can submit video analytics coming from their videos so creators, musicians, brands and businesses can view the performance and convert that to success coming to their brand identities. Before submissions are done, the user will have to verify the TikTok username the video (where the analytics would be coming from) is coming from. Once verification is successful, the username will be saved as a cached reference linked to their digital profile on Starlet. This data can also be accessed on Starlet by using the /stats command. Creator teams (moderators, administrators) with elevated Discord Guild permissions can view this data by doing /adminstats.
  • TikTok Creator ID: Similar to how TikTok usernames are saved, the TikTok Creator ID is also saved. When a user submits a video to Starlet, Starlet will verify if the video contains a TikTok User ID that has been verified with the user on Discord.
  • TikTok Video IDs: Users on Starlet can submit videos into Starlet, with those videos, their ID is saved so they can be used in the future to find new views and consistently update analytics, these analytics can be viewed by the user who submitted this information into Starlet, or by the content creators, musicians, artists, streamers, or creator teams that have worked with the user in question. All data collected from TikTok is publically accessible without authorization and shall and is treated as public data. Video IDs are removed out of circulation after the creator has decided to suspend compensation for the analytics from said Video ID. After that, the Video IDs will remain saved in Starlet databases and servers to make sure they aren't resubmitted in the future. To remove all your submitted Video IDs and digital profile from Starlet (Starlet databases & servers), you can request an account deletion. Statistics such as the video create time, as well as, view counts, like counts, comment counts, share counts may be collected and are continually updated.

By participating in this verification process, you allow Starlet to create or to add to an existing digital profile with your consented TikTok usernames and TikTok Creator IDs. In this digital profile, Starlet collects the Guild ID of the server where verification was done, as well as your Discord ID to identify this profile as yours. In the future, if you ever create a new Discord account for other activities, you can ask for an account transfer through transfer@starlet.gg. Account transfers are not commonly done and are only done with proper evidence that you are the true rightful owner of the accounts you are requesting to transfer to. Not all account transfer requests will be fulfilled. Privacy and protection comes first.

This digital profile can be viewed by using /stats on Starlet.

If you would like to permanently delete your digital profile on Starlet, you can initiate a deletion request through deletemyinfo@starlet.gg or join our Discord server for support on Starlet. For email requests, expected times are 14 days. For support through Discord, expected times are 7 days.
